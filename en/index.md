## Key Features
- **Feature 1**: One-Tap Tracking: Record routes instantly with high-precision GPS.
- **Feature 2**: Multi-Log Display: View past routes and notes alongside your current path.
- **Feature 3**: Route Planning: Create delivery areas or travel plans in advance.
- **Feature 4**: Notes & Photos: Pin locations with notes or photos for quick reference.
- **Feature 5**: Area Stats & Stop Timer: Count pins in an area or track idle time during deliveries.
- **Feature 6**: Route Sharing: Export/import routes via GPX/JSON or share via AirDrop.
- **Feature 7**: Arrival Alerts: Get audio notifications when approaching saved locations.
